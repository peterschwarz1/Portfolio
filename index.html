Relational Landscapes Website (12/2021) published with FH Potsdam.

Relational Landscapes is the conceptual framework for a design course at FH Potsdam that traces interconnectedness between Berlin-Brandenburg and Amazon and the Andes.
‍
On the website a landscape of Brandenburg and an area of the Andes are layered on top of each other. Users can move across the landscapes.

Synae (2019) a synesthetic video work at FH Potsdam.

In the work Synae I recorded with four students a sonic texture on a modular synthesizer to a color of their choice. Later each student animated their color to the corresponding sonic texture.
‍
The focus was on the emotional connection and perception of the color.
‍
Later I designed a kinetic type poster for a projection mapping event where the video was planned to be premiered.
